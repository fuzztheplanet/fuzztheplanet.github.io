About this place

I'm Skywhi and this is my personal website. I finally decided to jump onto the modern train and instead of managing my ~/notes/*.txt files locally they are now also dumped hosted "in the Cloud" (©). The opinions and views expressed on this website are solely my own because nobody else wanted them.

The Inconsolata fonts are from the Google Fonts project. The image for the RSS button was made by Laisa Islam Ani. Some content is published with the ReadTheOrg theme from org-html-themes by Fabrice Niessen. Many thanks to them for their great work!
