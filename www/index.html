Welcome to my little patch of internet!

This is where I spend my days hunting insects, sweet-talking ciphers, dancing the carioca, pondering questionable life decisions and arguing with Emacs as if we were in a long-term relationship neither of us can leave. In the midst of all this chaos, I aim to remain a humble student of the zen of pwn - seeking glimpses of enlightenment through suspicious-looking code, elusive CTF flags and sticky salted caramel treats.

Visitors be warned, this blog is not a polished magazine whatsoever; it is merely a patchwork of rants, experiments and unfinished work, written in plain language. posts hosts sporadic, one-off topics while the notes section contains longer pieces that might get updated over time (no promises, but optimism is free). Particularly shiny resources are hoarded under links. Information about this website and contact details can be found under about. What Emacs allows me to share of my life resides on my now page.

With this out of the way, I wish you happy reading!
