This is my now page (inspired by Derek Siver's /now movement and cym13's Stream of Consciousness). Here you will find a brief overview of my current activities and points of focus as well as some random, unsorted and unfiltered thoughts.

I am trying to push myself to document my on-going things and publish what could potentially be relevant to others. This could be my own dotfiles, some offsec / CTF knowledge, the things I use Org mode for, small utilities that I wrote and a few more. I have a lot of ideas in that direction, let's see if I find the time to make some progress over the summer.

Hopefully they can provide as much as some of my existing herbs, which consist mostly of mint, lavender, rosemary, oregano and parsley. In the end, the best I can hope for is that I kill them slower than my indoor plants.

I enjoy cooking and would like to start recording my favorite recipes in my own cookbook.

I'm listening to a lot of Hubert-Félix Thiéfaine and cumbia lately.
